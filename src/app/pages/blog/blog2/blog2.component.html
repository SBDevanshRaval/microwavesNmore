Step 1: Understand the Microwave Types

1. Solo Microwaves

  • Best for: Basic heating, reheating, and defrosting
  • Common Use: Small families, singles, or office use
  • Affordable and simple to use

2. Grill Microwaves

  • Best for: Grilling veggies, paneer, sandwiches, etc.
  • Includes: Heating + grill element
  • Great for apartment chefs

3. Convection Microwaves

  • Best for: Baking, roasting, crisping
  • Includes: Heating + grill + fan for even cooking
  • Ideal for all-in-one cooking solutions

Step 3: Must-Have Features to Look For

  • Auto Cook Menus – Preset buttons for pizza, popcorn, and more
  • Child Lock – Safety first, especially for families
  • Power Levels – Adjustable heat for precision
  • Timer & Defrost Options – Convenience and speed
  • Digital Display – Clear, modern, user-friendly
